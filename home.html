Why ICT and why it fits me

Money

Money plays a crucial role in my decision to pursue ICT as it ensures access to quality education, necessary resources, and the ability to invest in advanced technology for hands-on learning. It empowers me to fully immerse myself in the field, gaining valuable skills and experiences that will shape my future career.

Job opportunities

Job opportunities are a driving force behind my choice to study ICT. The field's promising employment prospects offer the potential for a rewarding career, financial stability, and a chance to contribute to cutting-edge technological advancements. This motivates me to invest in my education and seize the diverse opportunities that ICT has to offer.

Career change

Considering my background in the financial department, I see ICT as an opportunity for a career exchange that aligns with my interests and ambitions. Embracing technology not only opens doors to exciting prospects but also allows me to apply my financial acumen in innovative ways, fostering personal growth and professional satisfaction.
